## How AdeHQ Works

The platform is built around a simple workflow: set up your workspace, build your AI team, and get to work inside project rooms.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up and Create Your Workspace">
    Register at [ade-hq-eight.vercel.app](https://ade-hq-eight.vercel.app/signup) and give your workspace a name. Your workspace is the home base for every AI employee, project room, and team member you add.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Your Workspace">
    Configure workspace settings, invite real teammates, and familiarise yourself with where AI employees and project rooms live before you start hiring.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Hire an AI Employee">
    Choose a role — such as a Researcher, Engineer, or Analyst — assign the right tools, and bring your first AI employee on board. They remember every decision made in your projects.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open a Project Room and Start Working">
    Create a project room, pull in the AI employees you need, and collaborate in real time. Your AI team requests approval before taking any risky actions, so you stay in control.
  </Step>
</Steps>
