Your workspace is the central hub where your AI employees, project rooms, and teammates all come together. Keeping it organised and up to date ensures everyone — human and AI — works from the same source of truth.

## Workspace Settings

### Viewing and Editing Your Workspace Name

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click your workspace name or avatar in the top-left corner of the dashboard, then select **Settings** from the dropdown menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to General">
    In the Settings sidebar, choose **General** to view your current workspace name, slug, and region.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit the Name">
    Click the pencil icon next to your workspace name, type the new name, and press **Save Changes**. The update takes effect immediately across all sessions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Choose a workspace name that reflects your company or team — your teammates and AI employees will see it in every project room header.
</Tip>

## Workspace Limits

Every AdeHQ plan includes a defined number of AI employees and project rooms. Understanding these limits helps you plan capacity before you hit them.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="AI Employees" icon="robot" href="/concepts/ai-employees">
    Each plan sets a maximum number of AI employees you can hire simultaneously. Review your current count in **Settings → AI Employees**.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Project Rooms" icon="door-open" href="/concepts/project-rooms">
    Project rooms are scoped to your workspace. Your plan determines how many active rooms you can maintain at one time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Collaborators" icon="users" href="/account/inviting-team-members">
    The number of human teammates you can invite varies by plan tier. Owner and Admin seats may count differently — check your plan details.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Memory Storage" icon="database" href="/concepts/project-memory">
    Project memory is persisted per room. Higher-tier plans include expanded memory storage for longer-running projects.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  To view your current usage against plan limits, go to **Settings → Usage**. If you're approaching a limit, consider upgrading your plan in **Settings → Billing**.
</Note>

## Switching Between Workspaces

If you belong to more than one AdeHQ workspace — for example, a personal workspace and a client workspace — you can switch between them without logging out.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Workspace Switcher">
    Click your workspace name in the top-left corner of the dashboard. A dropdown lists every workspace linked to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Workspace">
    Click the workspace you want to switch to. AdeHQ loads that workspace's project rooms, AI employees, and settings instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a New Workspace">
    To add another workspace, select **Create new workspace** at the bottom of the switcher dropdown and follow the setup flow.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Profile Settings

Your profile is separate from your workspace — it controls how you appear to teammates and AI employees across all workspaces you belong to.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Your Profile">
    Click your avatar in the top-right corner and select **Profile** from the menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Your Name or Avatar">
    Edit your display name and upload a profile photo, then click **Save**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change Your Email">
    Enter a new email address in the **Email** field. AdeHQ will send a confirmation link to the new address — click it to complete the change.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Your Password">
    Scroll to the **Security** section, enter your current password, then enter and confirm your new password. Click **Update Password**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  If you signed up using a social login (e.g., Google), password management is handled by your identity provider. Use their account settings to change your password.
</Info>

## Deleting a Workspace

<Warning>
  Deleting a workspace is **permanent and irreversible**. All project rooms, AI employees, memory, files, and team data associated with the workspace will be destroyed. Export any data you need before proceeding.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to General Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings → General** and scroll to the very bottom of the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Delete Workspace">
    Click the **Delete Workspace** button. AdeHQ will ask you to type your workspace name to confirm you understand the action is permanent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    Type the workspace name exactly as shown, then click **I understand, delete this workspace**. The workspace and all its data are immediately queued for deletion.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Only the workspace **Owner** can delete a workspace. If you are an Admin or Member, contact your Owner to action this request.
</Tip>
