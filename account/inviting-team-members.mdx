AdeHQ is designed for teams — real people collaborating alongside AI employees inside shared project rooms. Inviting a teammate takes less than a minute, and they'll be working with your AI workforce straight away.

## Permission Levels

Before you send an invitation, choose the role that matches what your teammate needs to do.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Owner" icon="crown" href="/guides/team-collaboration">
    Full control over the workspace, including billing, deletion, and all settings. There is one Owner per workspace, and ownership can be transferred.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Admin" icon="shield-halved" href="/guides/team-collaboration">
    Can manage members, hire and configure AI employees, create project rooms, and adjust workspace settings. Cannot access billing or delete the workspace.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Member" icon="user" href="/guides/team-collaboration">
    Can work inside project rooms they are added to, interact with AI employees, and contribute to project memory. Cannot change workspace-level settings.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  Follow the principle of least privilege — grant teammates the lowest role that still lets them do their job. You can promote them later if needed.
</Tip>

## Inviting a Teammate

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Workspace Settings">
    Click your workspace name in the top-left corner of the dashboard and select **Settings** from the dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the Members Section">
    In the Settings sidebar, click **Members**. You'll see a list of everyone currently in the workspace, along with their roles and join dates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Invite Member">
    Click the **Invite Member** button in the top-right corner of the Members page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Their Email and Role">
    Type the teammate's email address into the input field. Use the **Role** dropdown to select **Admin** or **Member**. You can invite multiple people at once by adding more email addresses.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the Invitation">
    Click **Send Invite**. AdeHQ emails the invitation immediately. The invite link is valid for **72 hours**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Teammate Accepts and Joins">
    Your teammate receives an email with a **Join Workspace** button. If they already have an AdeHQ account, they're taken straight to the workspace. If not, they complete a brief sign-up at [ade-hq-eight.vercel.app/signup](https://ade-hq-eight.vercel.app/signup) and are then dropped into the workspace automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Pending invitations appear at the top of the Members list with a **Pending** badge until the teammate accepts or the invitation expires.
</Info>

## Resending or Cancelling an Invitation

If a teammate hasn't received their invite or the link has expired, you can resend it in a few clicks.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the Pending Invitation">
    Go to **Settings → Members** and locate the entry with the **Pending** badge.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resend or Cancel">
    Click the **⋯** (more options) menu next to the pending invite and choose either **Resend Invitation** or **Cancel Invitation**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Resending generates a fresh 72-hour link and invalidates the previous one. If your teammate can't find the email, ask them to check their spam or promotions folder.
</Note>

## Changing a Member's Role

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Members List">
    Navigate to **Settings → Members**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit the Role">
    Click the **⋯** menu next to the member's name and select **Change Role**. Choose the new role from the dropdown and confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Removing a Team Member

<Warning>
  Removing a member immediately revokes their access to the workspace and all project rooms. Any project memory or contributions they made remain intact — only their access is removed.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the Member">
    Go to **Settings → Members** and find the person you want to remove.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove Them">
    Click the **⋯** menu next to their name and select **Remove Member**. Confirm the action in the dialogue that appears.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If a teammate is leaving your organisation, remove them from the workspace before deactivating their corporate email — otherwise their pending session may persist briefly until it expires.
</Tip>
