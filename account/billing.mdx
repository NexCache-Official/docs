Your billing settings give you a clear view of what you're paying for, when you'll be charged next, and how to make changes to your subscription. Everything is managed in one place — no need to contact support for routine updates.

## Finding Your Billing Settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click your workspace name in the top-left corner of the dashboard and select **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    In the Settings sidebar, click **Billing**. You'll see your current plan, next renewal date, payment method on file, and a link to your invoice history.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Only the workspace **Owner** can view and manage billing. If you need to update payment details but aren't the Owner, ask them to make the change or transfer ownership to you first.
</Note>

## Understanding Plans

AdeHQ offers a free tier to help you get started, alongside paid plans that unlock more AI employees, project rooms, memory storage, and collaboration seats.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Free Tier" icon="seedling">
    Get started at no cost with a limited number of AI employees and project rooms. Ideal for individuals exploring what AdeHQ can do.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Paid Plans" icon="bolt">
    Unlock higher limits on AI employees, rooms, memory, and team seats. Billed monthly or annually — annual plans offer a discounted rate.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
  Specific plan features and pricing are displayed on the Billing page inside your workspace and on the AdeHQ pricing page. Limits and features may change as the product evolves.
</Info>

## Upgrading Your Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings → Billing** inside your workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Upgrade Plan">
    Click the **Upgrade** button next to your current plan. A plan comparison modal opens showing available tiers and their features.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a Plan and Billing Cycle">
    Select the plan that fits your needs. Toggle between **Monthly** and **Annual** billing to see the price difference. Annual billing is charged upfront for the full year.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Payment Details">
    If you don't have a payment method saved, enter your card details. AdeHQ uses industry-standard encryption to store payment information securely.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and Activate">
    Review the summary and click **Confirm Upgrade**. Your new plan limits take effect immediately — no restart or refresh needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  When you upgrade mid-cycle, AdeHQ prorates the charge so you only pay for the remaining days in your billing period at the new rate.
</Tip>

## Managing Payment Methods

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Billing Settings">
    Go to **Settings → Billing** and scroll to the **Payment Method** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a New Card">
    Click **Add Payment Method**, enter your card number, expiry, and CVC, then click **Save**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a Default">
    If you have multiple cards saved, click **Set as Default** next to the one you want to use for future renewals.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove a Card">
    Click the **⋯** menu next to any saved card and select **Remove**. You cannot remove your only payment method while on a paid plan.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing Invoices and Usage

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Billing Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings → Billing**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Invoice History">
    Scroll to the **Invoices** section. Each invoice shows the date, amount, and plan it covers. Click **Download** next to any invoice to save a PDF copy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Current Usage">
    Click the **Usage** tab to see how many AI employees, project rooms, and team seats you've consumed against your plan limits this billing cycle.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Cancelling Your Subscription

<Warning>
  Cancelling your paid plan downgrades your workspace to the free tier at the end of your current billing period. If your usage exceeds free-tier limits at that point, some AI employees and project rooms may become read-only until you reduce usage or resubscribe.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings → Billing**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Cancel Plan">
    Scroll to the bottom of the page and click **Cancel Subscription**. AdeHQ will ask you to confirm and may ask for brief feedback on why you're cancelling.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Cancellation">
    Click **Confirm Cancellation**. You'll receive an email confirmation, and your paid access continues until the end of the current billing period.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  For enterprise agreements, custom pricing, volume discounts, or invoicing arrangements, contact the AdeHQ team directly at **support@adehq.com**. The standard billing flow does not cover enterprise contracts.
</Note>
