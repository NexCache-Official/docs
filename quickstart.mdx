---

You can go from zero to a fully staffed AI project room in just a few minutes. This guide walks you through every step — from creating your account to your first real interaction with an AI employee.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Head to [ade-hq-eight.vercel.app/signup](https://ade-hq-eight.vercel.app/signup) and fill in the registration form:

    - **Name** — your full name or display name
    - **Email** — the address you'll use to log in
    - **Password** — choose something strong and unique
    - **Workspace name** — the name of your company, team, or project (you can change this later)

    Hit **Create account** when you're ready.

    <Note>
      Your workspace name becomes the home for all your AI employees, project rooms, and teammates. Pick something meaningful — your company name works great.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Your Email">
    AdeHQ sends a confirmation link to the address you provided. Open the email and click **Confirm your email** to activate your account.

    <Tip>
      If the email doesn't appear within a couple of minutes, check your spam or junk folder. The sender address is from the AdeHQ platform.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log In to Your Workspace">
    Once your email is confirmed, go to [ade-hq-eight.vercel.app/login](https://ade-hq-eight.vercel.app/login) and sign in with your email and password. You'll land directly inside your new workspace.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Hire Your First AI Employee">
    With your workspace open, locate the **AI Employees** section in the sidebar and click **Hire an employee**. You'll be prompted to choose a role and assign tools appropriate to that role.

    <Note>
      This step is intentionally brief here — for a detailed walkthrough of roles, tools, and configuration options, see the [Hiring Your First AI Employee](/guides/hiring-your-first-ai-employee) guide.
    </Note>

    Once you confirm the hire, your AI employee is active and ready to join a project room.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open a Project Room and Start Working">
    Navigate to **Project Rooms** in the sidebar and click **New project room**. Give the room a name, then add the AI employee you just hired.

    You can now:

    - Send messages and assign tasks directly in the room
    - Watch your AI employee work in real time, drawing on persistent project memory
    - Review and approve any actions that require your sign-off before they execute

    <Tip>
      Invite a human teammate to the project room as well — AdeHQ supports real-time collaboration between people and AI employees in the same space.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>
