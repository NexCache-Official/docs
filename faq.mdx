## General

<Accordion title="What is AdeHQ?">
  AdeHQ is your company's AI headquarters — a SaaS platform where you hire AI employees with real roles and tools, organise them into project rooms, and work alongside them in real time. Unlike a simple chatbot, AdeHQ gives each AI employee a persistent identity, a defined job function, and access to tools it needs to actually get work done. Your human teammates collaborate in the same workspace, making AdeHQ a genuine operating layer for AI-augmented teams.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is AdeHQ free to use?">
  Yes — AdeHQ offers a free tier so you can explore the platform without a credit card. The free tier includes a limited number of AI employees and project rooms. When you're ready to scale, paid plans unlock higher limits on employees, rooms, memory storage, and team seats. You can review plan options in **Settings → Billing** inside your workspace.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How is AdeHQ different from a regular chatbot?">
  A regular chatbot responds to a message and forgets everything the moment the conversation ends. AdeHQ is fundamentally different in three ways:

  1. **Persistent memory** — AI employees remember decisions, context, and outcomes across sessions using project memory tied to each room.
  2. **Real roles and tools** — each AI employee has a defined function (e.g., Marketing Lead, Operations Manager) and is equipped with tools to take real actions, not just generate text.
  3. **Team collaboration** — human teammates work in the same project rooms, alongside AI employees, in real time — making AdeHQ a collaborative workforce platform, not a single-user assistant.
</Accordion>

## AI Employees

<Accordion title="How many AI employees can I hire?">
  The number of AI employees you can hire depends on your plan. The free tier includes a small number of employee slots. Paid plans offer progressively higher limits. You can check your current count and remaining capacity under **Settings → Usage** at any time.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I give an AI employee a custom role?">
  Yes. When you hire a new AI employee, you define their role, responsibilities, and the tools they have access to. You can name the role anything that fits your organisation — "Head of Content," "Data Analyst," "Customer Success Lead" — and configure their behaviour to match the job description. You can update a role at any time from the AI employee's settings page.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do AI employees remember previous conversations?">
  Yes. AI employees use **project memory** — a persistent, structured memory tied to each project room. Whenever an important decision is made, context is established, or key information is shared, it's stored in the room's memory. The next time the AI employee is active in that room, it picks up exactly where things left off. This means you don't need to re-explain your project every session.
</Accordion>

## Project Rooms

<Accordion title="Can multiple people work in the same project room?">
  Yes — project rooms support real-time collaboration. Multiple human teammates and multiple AI employees can be active in the same room simultaneously. Everyone sees updates as they happen, powered by AdeHQ's Supabase-backed real-time infrastructure. This makes it possible to run a live working session where humans and AI employees work together on the same task.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I have multiple project rooms?">
  Yes. You can create as many project rooms as your plan allows. Each room has its own AI employees, project memory, and collaboration history — making rooms ideal for separating work by client, department, initiative, or any other structure that fits your team. Navigate between rooms from the left-hand sidebar on your dashboard.
</Accordion>

## Approvals & Safety

<Accordion title="What actions require approval?">
  AI employees are designed to act autonomously within their defined roles — but they pause and request your approval before taking actions that are irreversible, high-risk, or outside the scope of routine work. Examples include:

  - Sending external communications on your behalf
  - Deleting or overwriting important data
  - Making purchases or committing spend
  - Executing integrations that affect third-party systems

  You'll receive an in-app notification when an approval request is waiting. Review the proposed action, then approve or reject it with a single click.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I turn off approval requests?">
  Approval requests are a core safety feature designed to keep you in control. You can adjust the **approval threshold** for individual AI employees — setting them to request approval more or less frequently based on your trust level and the nature of their work. A full disable option is not available for high-risk action categories, ensuring there is always a human in the loop for consequential decisions.
</Accordion>

## Account & Billing

<Accordion title="How do I reset my password?">
  1. Go to the [AdeHQ login page](https://ade-hq-eight.vercel.app/login).
  2. Click **Forgot password?** below the sign-in form.
  3. Enter the email address associated with your account and click **Send Reset Link**.
  4. Check your inbox for a password reset email and click the link inside.
  5. Enter and confirm your new password, then click **Save**.

  If you signed up using a social login (e.g., Google), your password is managed by that provider — use their account settings to reset it.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I delete my account?">
  To delete your personal AdeHQ account, go to **Profile → Account** and scroll to the **Danger Zone** section. Click **Delete Account** and follow the confirmation steps. Note that if you are the Owner of one or more workspaces, you must either transfer ownership or delete those workspaces before your account can be removed. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
</Accordion>
